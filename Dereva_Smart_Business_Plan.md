## MARKET ANALYSIS

### The Problem

#### For Driving Schools & NTSA (The Fraud Crisis)

Following undercover exposés (e.g., BBC's "Kenya's Killer Roads") and NTSA crackdowns, major schools face intense scrutiny over instructors issuing licenses to students who skip practical hours. Manual logbooks are easily forged, putting schools' NTSA licenses at risk.

#### For Students

Learner drivers pay premium fees for practicals but lack modern theory support. They rely on outdated PDFs, YouTube, and fragmented quiz apps. The process of navigating eCitizen to apply for a PDL (Provisional Driving License) or book a test is disjointed from the actual learning process.

#### Licensing Friction

The administrative burden of applying for licenses through eCitizen is disconnected from the training experience, creating confusion and delays.

### Market Size & Opportunity

- **700+ NTSA-approved driving schools** across Kenya
- **Thousands of new learner drivers** enrolling monthly
- Growing demand for PSV and professional driver licenses
- Increasing smartphone penetration in urban and rural areas

### Competitive Landscape (As of Feb 2026)

#### Existing Solutions

1. **Zemala Tech & Mabtech Apps**
   - Static text notes
   - Outdated UX
   - Purely passive reading experience

2. **AA Kenya Test App**
   - Basic quizzes
   - Lacks full curriculum breakdown
   - No B2B digital logbook

3. **NTSA Official App**
   - Purely an eCitizen administrative tool
   - Zero educational or training capabilities

#### The Gap

No existing app provides an "Audit-Proof" GPS-verified logbook for schools, or an interactive 3D Model Town / AI Tutor for students. Birosoft Enterprises' Dereva Smart fills this critical gap in the market.

## PRODUCT & SERVICES

### The B2B2C Model

Dereva Smart operates on a dual-market approach, serving both driving schools and individual learners with complementary products.

### A. For Driving Schools (B2B Admin & Instructor Dashboard)

#### Audit-Proof NTSA Logbook

Instructors log yard/road hours via a mobile app using QR check-ins and GPS-stamping. This guarantees to NTSA auditors that the student actually drove the vehicle for the required hours.

**Key Features:**
- Real-time GPS tracking and verification
- QR code check-in system
- Tamper-proof digital records
- Automatic hour calculation and validation

#### Student Management

- Bulk import students
- Assign students to specific instructors
- Category-based organization (A1-A3, B1-B3, C, D, E, F, G)
- Progress tracking and reporting

#### Automated Reporting

Generate NTSA-compliant progress reports instantly, reducing administrative burden and ensuring audit readiness.

#### White-Labeling (Premium Tier)

Premium tier allows schools to have a branded student portal (e.g., "Petanns Smart" powered by Dereva), enhancing their brand while leveraging our technology.

### B. For Learner Drivers (B2C Mobile App)

#### eCitizen & PDL Integration

Direct links and guides to apply for the mandatory PDL (KSh 650) via eCitizen straight from the onboarding screen, streamlining the licensing process.

#### School-Synced Study Plan

Syncs with the user's registered driving school, unlocking targeted modules (e.g., Common Core Units) based on what the instructor taught that day.

#### Gemini 3.1 Pro AI Tutor

Real-time conversational AI supporting both English and Swahili.

**Example Queries:**
- "Nifafanulie human factors kwa Kiswahili"
- "Explain the 3-point turn"
- "What are the stopping distances?"

#### Interactive Theory

100% NTSA curriculum aligned content featuring:
- Short instructional videos
- AR road signs
- Interactive drag-and-drop 3D Model Town board
- Visual learning aids

#### Mock Testing & Certification

- Auto-generates 50-question NTSA replica tests
- Enforces the strict 80% NTSA pass mark
- Unlocks digital "Ready for NTSA" badge upon passing
- Shareable Certificate of Competence

#### Offline Mode

Fully downloadable theory modules for low-data, rural users, ensuring accessibility across Kenya.

## RISK MANAGEMENT

### Risk Assessment & Mitigation Strategies

| Risk Factor | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------------|--------|-------------------|
| **School Resistance (Fear of losing students)** | High | Strict messaging: "We are a theory supplement, NOT a replacement." Partner contracts ensure we push practical bookings to them. Position as a tool that increases their revenue and compliance. |
| **Low Tech Literacy among Instructors** | Medium | Keep the B2B app extremely simple (Big buttons, QR code scans, WhatsApp integration). Provide in-person onboarding and ongoing support. Create video tutorials in Swahili. |
| **Data Privacy & Compliance** | High | Strict adherence to Kenya's Data Protection Act (DPA, 2019) regulated by the ODPC. Secure handling of student IDs, PDL data, and medical records. Regular security audits. |
| **NTSA Curriculum Changes** | Medium | Utilize Gemini to quickly adapt content to curriculum updates. Maintain direct communication with NTSA. Build flexible content management system. |
| **Competition from Established Players** | Medium | Focus on unique GPS-verified logbook feature. Emphasize AI tutor and 3D Model Town. Build strong school partnerships early. |
| **Payment Collection Challenges** | Low | Leverage M-Pesa's trusted infrastructure. Offer flexible payment plans. Provide clear value demonstration. |
| **Technical Infrastructure Failures** | Medium | Use reliable cloud providers (Firebase/Supabase). Implement redundancy. Regular backups. Offline mode for critical features. |

### Compliance Framework

#### NTSA Alignment
- Utilize Gemini to map 100% of content directly to LN 28 of 2020
- Enforce the 80% pass mark required by NTSA for internal assessments
- Regular curriculum audits and updates

#### Data Protection
- Compliance with Kenya's Data Protection Act (DPA, 2019)
- Registration with Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC)
- Secure data storage and transmission
- Clear privacy policies and user consent
